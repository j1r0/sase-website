Who We Are

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is a national non-profit organization in the United States with a mission to empower individuals of Asian heritage who are pursuing careers in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields. SASE provides a range of programs and opportunities designed to support its members in their professional development and help them excel in their careers.
At the Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University @ Daytona Beach (ERAU-DB), we offer an incredible variety of opportunities for students looking to elevate their college experience. in many activities, social events, professional development, volunteering, and fundraising!
