Who We Are

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is a national non-profit organization in the United States with a mission to empower individuals of Asian heritage who are pursuing careers in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields. SASE provides a range of programs and opportunities designed to support its members in their professional development and help them excel in their careers.

At the Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University @ Daytona Beach (ERAU-DB) chapter of SASE, we take pride in elevating the college experience. Our vibrant and tight-knit community enables us to organize a diverse range of activities that cater to various interests. From professional development workshops and leadership seminars to volunteering and social events, we strive to create an engaging environment filled with inclusivity.

At ERAU-DB, our chapter of SASE is more than just a professional development and diversity club. It is a warm and welcoming place where you can experience camaraderie, support, and belonging. Join us, and together, we can soar like Eagles and reach for the stars!
