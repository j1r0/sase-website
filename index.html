Who We Are

Join us, and together, we can soar like Eagles and reach for the stars!

STEM

SASE's mission is to empower individuals of Asian heritage who are pursuing careers in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields. Our chapter achieves this by offering mentorship and fostering a supportive community for our members' advancements and success.

Community

Our chapter of SASE is more than just a professional development and diversity club. It is a warm and welcoming place where you can experience camaraderie, support, and belonging. We provide opportunities for our members to make a difference in our community.

Diversity & Inclusion

Our vibrant and tight-knit community enables us to organize a diverse range of activities that cater to various interests. Individuals from all backgrounds are welcome to find opportunities to connect, learn, and contribute to our melting pot of experiences and perspectives.

Professional Development

We provide a range of programs and opportunities designed to support its members in their professional development and help them excel in their careers. From on-campus networking events to off-campus professional conventions, we are dedicated to ensure that our members are equipped with invaluable resources and experiences.
